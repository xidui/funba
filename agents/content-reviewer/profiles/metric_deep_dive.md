# Metric Deep Dive Review Profile

Use this profile for metric-analysis or metric deep-dive SocialPosts.

Apply the full generic checklist in `agents/content-reviewer/AGENTS.md`.

Extra emphasis:

- Verify the metric definition, scope, season/window, and leaderboard interpretation before editing prose.
- Keep metric URLs aligned with the exact metric key and season/window discussed in the article.
- Do not turn a season or playoff ranking into a one-game claim.
- If the article explains a ranking movement, verify that movement evidence is available from the issue or Funba APIs.
